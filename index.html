Management

A dedicated team on every event.

Five roles. One accountable chain — the unified safety net legacy planning never had.

01

Planner

Designs the full event architecture — vision, budget, vendors, and timeline — and remains your strategic lead throughout.

02

Coordinator

Manages every vendor relationship and confirmation cycle, keeping all moving pieces aligned to a single schedule.

03

Production Manager

Owns logistics, load-in, and technical execution — power, staging, permits, and contingency activation.

04

On-Site Manager

Runs the day itself: cueing the timeline, directing vendors, and resolving anything in real time.

05

On-Site Staff

Hands-on support for guests and vendors — setup, service, and strike, handled invisibly.
